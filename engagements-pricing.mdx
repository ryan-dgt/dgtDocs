## Why it's split this way

Most agencies bundle the build into a long retainer, which hides what you're actually paying for and makes leaving expensive. Separating them keeps both sides honest: the build is a fixed piece of work with a fixed price, and the monthly partnership has to keep justifying itself every single month.

It also means you can stop after the build. The system is documented and it's yours, so it keeps working whether we're in the room or not.

## How pricing works

The work is priced before we start and doesn't change unless the scope does, and scope only changes if you change it. Ad spend is separate: it's always paid by you, directly to the platforms, in your own accounts. We never mark up media, and we take no percentage of spend, so our advice about your budget is never influenced by our fee.
