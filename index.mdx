## Who we are

do good things is a [growth marketing studio](https://www.dogoodthings.co.nz) based out of Queenstown, New Zealand, working with consumer brands across NZ, Australia, and beyond. We design, build, and manage AI-backed growth marketing systems that bring performance creative, paid ads, AI Search/SEO, and conversion together in one workflow. Engagements start with a [free audit](/the-audit), are [priced from $3,500 per month](/engagements-pricing), and are structured around outcomes, not deliverables. We become the fractional head of growth within your brand.

## Who we're a good fit for

- **You're a marketing leader or founder of a consumer brand.** Most of the brands we work with have already done growth marketing in pieces: ads with one agency, creative with another, email handled in-house. Each piece worked, sort of, on its own. That's where we integrate best. The pieces exist, and what's missing is one system connecting them.
- **You want senior operators doing the work.** You'll work directly with the people who run the growth marketing system.
- **You value a problem-solving, get-it-done approach.** When something is losing money, you hear it in that week's update, not the monthly review.
- **You're comfortable being accountable to numbers.** Because we own outcomes together, we'll ask you to agree on what success looks like up front, in writing, so we're all on the same page.

<Warning>
  **Where we're probably not the best fit**

  We'd rather be upfront early than waste your time. If what you need right now is a brand campaign with no performance accountability, a one-off design project, or extra hands to execute a plan that's already set, we're probably not the best value for you. We'll say so on the first call, and we're happy to point you toward people who do that work brilliantly. Not sure which side of the line you're on? Ask us.
</Warning>
